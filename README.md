# Research Paper - Transportation Optimization

## Title
A New Approach For Solving Integer Interval 
Transportation Problem With Mixed Constraints

## Published In
- Journal: IOP Journal of Physics Conference Series
- Volume: 1377, Article: 012043
- Year: 2019

## Abstract
Proposed an optimization method using interval 
numbers to solve transportation problems with 
mixed constraints. Used VAM and MODI methods 
to minimize total transportation cost and 
delivery time without converting to classical 
transportation problem.
